Breaking News: Mount Rebero Becomes Top Tourist Spot

Recently, Mount Rebero has become a popular tourist destination in Kigali. This happened after many years of hard work from both the city officials and people living in Rebero. The widespread popularity has tremendously increased tourism in the area. The Mount has been a sleeping beauty for years, because it was only known by hikers, and those who enjoyed fresh air and quietness, and now new reports from the Rwanda Development Board authorities are confirming the surge in visitors in recent months. Authorities and local residents are said to be working on building the tallest tower in Kigali and it is said it will be completed by 2030.
